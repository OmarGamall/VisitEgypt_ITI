Alexandria: Egypt's Coastal Gem

Founded by Alexander the Great, Alexandria is a city of high culture and Mediterranean charm. From its legendary ancient lighthouse to its modern world-class library, it remains a beacon of knowledge and beauty.

Bibliotheca Alexandrina

One of the most famous libraries in the world, combining modern architecture with ancient history. It is a center for knowledge, culture, and education.

Qaitbay Citadel

A historic fortress located on the Mediterranean coast. It offers amazing sea views and reflects the military history of Alexandria.

Alexandria Corniche

A long coastal road where visitors can enjoy walking, sea breeze, and beautiful sunset views.
